# Dice Roll Sound

## Add Your Sound

Place your dice roll sound file here: `/public/dice-roll.mp3`

### Requirements
- **Format**: MP3, WAV, or OGG (MP3 recommended for compatibility)
- **Duration**: 1-3 seconds
- **File size**: Under 500KB recommended

### Where to Get Free Dice Sounds

1. **Freesound.org** - https://freesound.org/search/?q=dice+roll
2. **Zapsplat.com** - https://www.zapsplat.com/sound-effect-category/dice/
3. **Mixkit.co** - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/

### Testing

After adding your sound:
1. Refresh the web app
2. Click "Roll Dice"
3. Sound should play during the roll animation

### No Sound?

If you don't want sound, the app works fine without it. The `dice-roll.mp3` file is optional.
